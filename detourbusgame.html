Role: Lead Game Designer & Scripter

A casual, VR, construction-comedy game where players build winding highways around themselves to guide the Flowers family on a roadtrip across a post-infrastructure America. 

Official Site Play the Prototype!

My Work (so far):

  • Developed mechanic prototypes which deepened the dynamics of the core game loop
  • Assisted team perforce training
  • Concepted, documented, prototyped, and iterated various mechanics and features throughout the game.
  • Designed and greyboxed 5 of 8 levels, implemented necessary gameplay features in each
  • Iterated level design and mechanic feel based on external playtest feedback
  • Reviewed work and collaborated with every discipline on the project to give critical actionable feedback based on the design pillars, gameplay aesthetics, previous experience, design best practices, and playtest feedback
  • Documented all features in a Macro GDD as well as an old school GDD ("design bible" more useful for final feature implementation)
