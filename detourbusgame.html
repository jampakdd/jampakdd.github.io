Lead Game Designer:

My Contributions to Detour Bus included, but are not limited to;
- Macro and Micro Design Documentation (drafting and iterating)
- Mechanic Prototyping
- Greyboxed Level Designs
- Various System Implementations
- Visual, Haptic, and Auditory Gameplay Feedback

Blurb/Blog:

For this project, while I was officially a lead designer, I worked closely with the director, producer, engineering team, art team, audio designer, usability team, narrative designers, and fellow gameplay designer to make Detour Bus the most fun and polished it could be while learning a lot about implementing interactions and designs for VR. Had tons of fun developing this project and I hope to continue to design for VR going into the future.

Detour Bus will be launching for Steam VR and the Oculus platform, Spring 2021.
