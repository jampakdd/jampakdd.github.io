Blurb Blog:

I learned so much about myself, my goals as a game developer, and what it really takes to make a game a success on this project. The amount of time and energy each and every person on this team put into the project is evident in both its beauty, scope, and reach. We were not only lucky enough to very successfully kickstart this project but also pitch and partner with White Thorn games to publish our game on Steam, Nintendo Switch, Xbox, and PlayStation.

Cant wait for our careers to intersect again to work with these incredible people and passionate developers.
