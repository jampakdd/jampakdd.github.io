Rules on the box!

Make it at home!

All you need is a standard children's fridge magnet set a whiteboard and a few friends!

President's Cabinet

3-4 Players
18+

Setup

  • Divide players into 4 parties.
  • Shuffle all magnets on a whiteboard in the starting state.
  • The President in the first round is determined by all players voting on who they see as most likely to become President of the United States. If there is a tie, the top candidates should engage in a rock, paper, scissors tournament until there is one winner!

Board Setup

How to Play

  • President’s Cabinet is played in a series of rounds that the President Times.
  • Each round runs as follows:
  • The President draws a random issue from the World Issue card pile.
  • Once the Presidential Party team reads the World Issue card out loud, the remaining 3 parties have 60 seconds to compose a proposed solution to the issue with the letters on the board. Timed by the president.
  • After the 60 seconds are up, the Presidential leader selects their favorite proposal. This team receives the World Issue card, indicating a successful proposal.
  • The title of President is passed onto a random party and the next round begins.
  • Letters can not be held in the player's hands without use.
  • Letters can not be collected for the sake of collecting.

How to Win

  • First party to successfully pass 3 proposals by the president wins!

Penalties - take effect IMMEDIATELY

  • If a party steals a letter from another party, everyone in the thief’s party must perform 5 jumping jacks before resuming the composition of their solution.
  • If a party accidentally knocks a letter off the board and is not caught before it hits the ground or the whiteboard tray, everyone in that party must do 1 push up.
